## For AI Agents

### Working In This Directory

- Research notes are **append-friendly**: update with new findings, new papers, new benchmarks. A "Last updated" date in the front is useful.
- When a research note produces an actionable decision, promote the decision to a dated spec in `../specs/` — don't bury load-bearing choices here.
- Cite papers by arXiv ID + a short blurb. Avoid paywalled-only refs.
- French or English are both fine here (sota-training-2026 is in French). Specs should be English; research can be whichever language the author thought in.

### Testing Requirements

No executable tests.

### Common Patterns

- Tables: pertinence rating (★ to ★★★★★), MLX-support column, paper/repo links.
- Pipeline recommendations are phase-numbered (Phase 1 / Phase 2 / ...) to match the plans in `../plans/`.
- arXiv IDs are bare (e.g. `2405.14734`), not full URLs — links are generated by the reader.

## Dependencies

### Internal
- Research notes frequently seed specs in `../specs/` and plans in `../plans/`.
- The 2026-04-16 landscape doc is cited by the 04-16 pivot spec.

### External
- arXiv references only. No code execution in these docs.
